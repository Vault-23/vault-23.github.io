2. Principles

The protection of your personal data is of great importance to us. We process your personal data in accordance with the Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (nFADP) and, where applicable, the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). In this privacy policy, we inform you about which personal data we process and for what purpose.

3. Data Collected

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact data: Name, email address, phone number when you use our contact form or register.
  • Property data: Address, property type, area, year of construction, and other details about your property that you provide as part of a valuation or sales mandate.
  • Usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, time spent, and referrer URL when visiting our website.
  • Communication data: Content of messages you send us via the contact form, email, or other channels.

4. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Providing our services (property valuation, marketing, lead qualification)
  • Responding to your inquiries and communicating with you
  • Improving and developing our website and services
  • Compliance with legal obligations

5. AI-Powered Data Processing

immoMatch.ai uses AI agents to create property valuations, optimize listings, and qualify prospects. These automated processes are based on data provided by you and publicly available market data. According to Art. 21 nFADP, you have the right to be informed about fully automated individual decisions and to assert your point of view.

6. Data Hosting and Storage Location

Our infrastructure is operated on Microsoft Azure Switzerland North (Zurich). All personal data is stored and processed in Switzerland. Data is generally not transferred abroad unless adequate safeguards exist under Art. 16 ff. nFADP.

7. Disclosure to Third Parties

We only share your personal data with third parties in the following cases:

  • To valuation service providers (e.g., IAZI, PriceHubble, Wüest Partner) for creating property valuations, in anonymized or pseudonymized form where possible
  • To real estate portals (e.g., Homegate, ImmoScout24) for publishing your listings, provided you have issued a sales mandate
  • To authorities when we are legally obligated to do so
  • To processors who support us in providing our services (e.g., hosting providers), with appropriate contractual agreements

8. Contact Form and Formspree

Our contact form uses the service Formspree (Formspree Inc., USA) to process your messages. Your submitted data is transmitted to Formspree servers. Formspree commits to maintaining adequate data protection standards. Further information can be found in Formspree's privacy policy.

9. Cookies and Web Analytics

Our website currently does not use any tracking cookies or analytics tools. No personal usage data is shared with third parties.

10. Your Rights

Under the nFADP, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: You may request information about whether and which personal data we process about you.
  • Right to rectification: You may request the correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to erasure: You may request the deletion of your personal data, provided no legal retention obligation exists.
  • Right to data portability: You may request the release of your personal data in a common electronic format.
  • Right to object: You may object to the processing of your data at any time.

To exercise your rights, please contact: info@immomatch.ai

11. Retention Period

We store your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purposes or as required by legal retention obligations. Contact inquiries are typically deleted after 12 months, unless an ongoing business relationship exists.

12. Data Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These include encrypted data transmission (TLS/SSL), access controls, and regular security audits.

13. Changes

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. The current version is published on our website.
