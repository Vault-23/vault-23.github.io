About Us

The Future of Property Sales

We believe nobody should pay CHF 30,000 in commission for a service that technology delivers better and cheaper.

Our Mission

immoMatch.ai aims to fundamentally transform Swiss property sales. Over CHF 1.2 billion in broker commissions flow annually into a service that can be 80 to 90% automated. We bridge the gap between expensive full-service brokers and limited self-sale portals.

With specialized AI agents for valuation, marketing, buyer screening, and contract preparation, we offer full-service quality at a fraction of the cost. Only 1% success commission instead of the usual 2 to 3%.

Fairness

The Founder

immoMatch.ai is founded by a technology expert with three decades of experience. His path led from the startup world through the Swiss financial industry to a global software corporation.

His career began at one of Switzerland's largest retailers, where he contributed to the early days of the online shop and worked on one of the country's best-known customer loyalty programmes. Even then, he learned how to build digital products for millions of users.

As CTO of eventlokale.ch, he built Switzerland's largest event portal from its founding in 2008 until its sale, with over 3,000 daily users. Building a Swiss platform from zero to market leadership, this experience shapes the approach of immoMatch.ai.

He then moved into Swiss finance, where he contributed to the development of a leading derivatives platform at a renowned Zurich private bank. A platform used by institutional investors worldwide today. Here he deepened his understanding of regulated markets, data security and scalable financial architectures.

He currently leads the development of tailored business solutions for enterprise clients at one of the largest American software companies. This proximity to enterprise technology, cloud infrastructure and AI innovation feeds directly into the technological DNA of immoMatch.ai.

The idea for immoMatch.ai was born from personal frustration when buying property in Switzerland. Unfriendly agents who are barely reachable. Unreliable arrangements. Overpriced commissions that bear no relation to the service provided. There had to be a better way.

The combination of startup experience, financial industry know-how and enterprise technology is the strategic advantage of immoMatch.ai. The biggest changes rarely come from insiders. They come from technologists who question outdated models from the outside.
